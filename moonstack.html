Overview

Moonstack is a next-gen prediction market built on Thetanuts Finance's options protocol, blending serious DeFi infrastructure with a memetic, gamified user experience.

The Moongle NFT System

To enter the arena, users must mint a "Moongle"—a personalized Beagle NFT. This isn't a random mint; Moonstack uses AI to analyze your current profile picture, extracting your unique vibe to generate a Beagle with matching traits.

Dynamic Evolution

Your Moongle evolves with you. As you engage with the platform and increase your user activity, your Beagle gets boosted with rarer generations and traits—unlocking dynamic upgrades like capes, glowing auras, and unique fur colors. It's where on-chain activity meets on-chain drip.

Technical Achievements
  • Engineered and deployed a gas-optimized ERC-721 smart contract on the Base L2 network
  • Implemented custom royalty standards for secondary market sustainability
  • Developed a responsive minting dApp using Next.js and Ethers.js
  • Built dynamic metadata generation to enhance user engagement
  • Managed end-to-end product lifecycle from smart contract architecture to mainnet deployment
