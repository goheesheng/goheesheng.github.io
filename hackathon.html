Overview

As an organizer of the Blockchain@NTU x Base Hackathon, I helped bring together 200 builders from around the world to Singapore for a 10-day intensive blockchain development event. The hackathon finals were held at AWS Singapore on August 26, 2025, showcasing innovative solutions in blockchain, artificial intelligence, and emerging technologies.

Event Highlights
  • 200 builders from around the world
  • 10 days of intensive development
  • Finals held at AWS Singapore office
  • Focus on blockchain, AI, and emerging technologies
  • Top prizes won by NTU CCTF Master of Science in Blockchain students
My Role as Organizer

  • Sponsor relationship management
  • Participant outreach and community building
  • Technical mentorship and support
  • Logistics and venue coordination
